The Dr*Co (THE DRYER COMPANY) was established in the year 2020 with an eye to manufacture high performance Clothes Dryers. The company's headquarters is situated in Sarnia, Ontario, Canada, and it has manufacturing plants across the whole country.

Our organization delivers different types of Clothes Dryers to its customers with a solid commitment to quality. They undergo strict quality checks which ensures the product's strength, capacity, speed, and it guarantee great performance output at a low operating cost.

We have a well-designed customer friendly web application which allows the customers to get hold of our products from their home itself. We also run certain rewards programs which offers the customers the products with great deals. Delivery of the product is made available to each corner of the country without any extra charges.

In addition to this, our commitment has helped us generate a huge client base. Consumers are also guaranteed a fine post purchase experience. We provide on-site service maintenance for our products. Our warranty policies are thoughtfully designed as per the customer needs. Customers can use the online application to register for any service.

We make products that save energy and greatly reduce pollutants. This is our global responsibility towards sustainable development. Owing to this faith, we have been introducing up to date Clothes Dryers with brand new innovations, without any compromise in quality and performance.

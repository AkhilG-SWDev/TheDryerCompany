Our story

Originally The Dryer Company was known as DC Ltd, and started operations in Canada in 2015 in collaboration with IFB of US. In 2020, it started operating individually with the production of fully-automatic cloth dryer machines and got renamed as The DR*CO, The Dryer Company. The factory is based at Sarnia Industrial Estate, Ontario, Canada.
